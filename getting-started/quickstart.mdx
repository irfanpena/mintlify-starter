This guide will walk you through the essential steps to get started with our scraping platform. Whether you’re new to web scraping or already experienced, you’ll learn how to create and run your first scraper in just a few minutes.
<Steps>
<Step title="Create an Account">
If you don’t have an account yet, [sign up here](https://v3.app.mrscraper.com/auth/register), then log in to your dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Create a New Scraper">
1. After logging in, go to the **Scraper** page by clicking the **triangle icon** in the left sidebar.  
2. Click **New Scraper +** at the top to create a new scraper.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/ai-mode-scraper/step-1.png)
</Frame>

3. Enter the URL of the website you want to scrape.

<Frame>
  ![](/images/ai-mode-scraper/step-2.png)
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="View Your Results">
    1. Click **Start Scraping →** to begin.  
    2. Once the process completes, you can view or download your results in multiple formats—**HTML**, **JSON**, **Table**, or **Graph**.

    <Frame>
    ![](/images/ai-mode-scraper/step-3.png)
    </Frame>
</Step>

</Steps>
