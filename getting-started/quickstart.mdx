# Quickstart Guide

Welcome to the MrScraper Quickstart Guide! This guide will walk you through the essential steps to get started with our powerful scraping platform. Whether you're new to web scraping or looking to enhance your skills, this guide will help you set up and run your first scraper in no time.

<Steps>
    <Step title="Create an Account">
       You can create an account [here](https://v3.app.mrscraper.com/auth/register) if you don't have one already. Then, log in to your account.
    </Step>
    <Step title="Create a New Scraper">
       Once logged in, navigate to the Scraper page by clicking the **triangle icon** on the left panel. Click the **New Scraper +** button on top to create a new scraper.

    </Step>
