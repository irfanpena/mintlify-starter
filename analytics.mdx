## Overview

MrScraper Analytics provides a comprehensive dashboard to monitor and analyze your scraping activities. Gain insights into your scraping performance, track success rates, and identify areas for improvement.

<Frame>
    ![](/images/analytic.png)
</Frame>

## Features

- **Dashboard**: Visual representation of your scraping activities, including success rates, error rates, and data volume.
- **Domain filtering**: Analyze scraping performance on a per-domain basis.
- **Time range selection**: Customize the time frame for your analytics data.
- **Timezone support**: View analytics data in your preferred timezone.
