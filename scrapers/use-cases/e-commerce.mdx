This guide shows how to use **MrScraper AI Scraper** to collect product information from **Amazon Marketplace**.


## Scenario

You want to collect data on the **top-selling laptops** on Amazon to analyze market trends or compare pricing.
For this example, youâ€™ll scrape data from the following category page:
[https://www.amazon.com/s?k=laptops](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=laptops)

Your goal is to extract:

* Product name
* Price
* Rating
* Number of reviews
* Product link

This use case helps you understand how to gather structured e-commerce data using AI Scraper without writing any code.

## Steps to Scrape
<Steps>
<Step title="Open the Scraper">

1. Log in to your **MrScraper dashboard**.
2. Go to **Scraper** > Click **New Scraper**.
<Frame>
  ![](/images/ecommerce/2.png)
</Frame>
3. Paste this link into the URL box:

   ```
   https://www.amazon.com/s?k=laptops
   ```
4. Click **Start Scraping**.
<Frame>
  ![](/images/ecommerce/3.png)
</Frame>
</Step>
<Step title="Write the Prompt">

1. In the prompt box, enter this instruction:

> Scrape the top 3 laptop products on this page and return the following information in JSON format:
>
> * product_name
> * price
> * rating
> * number_of_reviews
> * product_link

<Frame>
  ![](/images/ecommerce/4.png)
</Frame>
